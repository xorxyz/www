There is hardware supporting Dreamspace in the material plane ("the Substrate"). It fades away and requires maintenance.

Players need to ensure their machine is able to support their virtual projection by feeding it electricity and not shorting it.

Hypermachines can host an OS stack and have I/O devices connected to them.

Characters can have up to three machines at a given time.

- Local time (21:31 CEST)
- Uptime (up 9 weeks, 3 days, 12 hours, 57 minutes)
- CPU temperature (36.10°C)
- CPU load average (12%)
- Solar panel active (no)
- Battery charging (no)
- Battery capacity (64%)
- Power usage from battery (1.17W)
- Current draw from battery (.26Ah)
- Battery voltage (3.83V)
